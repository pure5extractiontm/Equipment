What is PURE5™ cannabis extraction technology?

PURE5™ uses advanced R134a technology for eco-friendly, efficient extraction of full-spectrum cannabis concentrates. Our system is designed for high purity and quality, optimizing yields while minimizing environmental impact.

How does the R134a technology in PURE5™ extraction systems work?

R134a technology is a refrigerant-based extraction method used by PURE5™ systems. It enables superior extraction with minimal heat, preserving the full range of cannabinoids, terpenes, and other compounds for high-quality concentrates.

What are the benefits of using PURE5™ extraction systems over traditional methods?

The PURE5™ system provides several advantages, including faster extraction times, higher yields, reduced environmental impact, and preservation of the full spectrum of cannabinoids and terpenes. The use of R134a refrigerant is both energy-efficient and eco-friendly.

Are PURE5™ extraction systems suitable for both small and large-scale operations?

Yes, PURE5™ extraction systems are designed to meet the needs of both small-scale operations and large commercial producers. They are scalable, efficient, and flexible, making them an ideal choice for diverse business sizes.

How do I maintain a PURE5™ extraction system?

Maintaining a PURE5™ system involves regular checks and cleaning of components such as the condenser and extraction chambers. Our detailed user manuals and dedicated customer support are always available to help ensure optimal performance.
