Why Choose R134a Technology for Cannabis Extraction?

Our R134a extraction system offers several key advantages over traditional extraction methods. By using a refrigerant-based process, we are able to operate at lower temperatures and pressures, which results in the preservation of delicate cannabinoids and terpenes. This not only enhances the flavor and potency of the extract but also minimizes thermal degradation, ensuring a superior product for consumers.

  • Non-flammable and Non-toxic: R134a is a safe, FDA-approved solvent commonly used in food and pharmaceutical applications. It offers a non-toxic and non-flammable solution for cannabis extraction.
  • Energy-Efficient: Our systems are designed to optimize extraction cycles, reducing power consumption and increasing the overall energy efficiency of your operations.
  • Eco-Friendly: The closed-loop design of our systems ensures zero emissions, providing a sustainable solution with complete solvent recovery. This makes PURE5™ extraction systems one of the most eco-friendly options available.
